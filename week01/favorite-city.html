My Favorite City

Brussels is the capital city of Belgium and the administrative heart of the country. It is also considered the de facto capital of the European Union. The city is home to major EU institutions, including the European Commission. Brussels is known for its stunning central square, the Grand Place, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. A famous landmark in the city is the quirky statue called Manual Pis. Brussels is widely celebrated for its delicious waffles, chocolates, and fries. The city has two official languages: French and Dutch. It features impressive architecture, blending medieval buildings with modern designs like the Actium. Brussels has a vibrant cultural scene with museums, art galleries, and international festivals. Its diverse population reflects its role as an international political and cultural hub.
